Introduction

My name is David Dai. I was born on January 18, 2005. I was born in China. I don't know why my family gave me this name. My family consist of my mother, my father, and two little brothers.

Elementary School

The first elementary school that I went to was Haines. The school was great and I liked it. The amazing thing about that was the fact that a person from that classroom is also in Whitney Young. My first memory was a bad one. I remember being late for school and the teacher yelled at me. For vacation, I went back to China to visit my family and relatives.

Middle School

For middle school, I went to Healy. That was where I met my most recent friends. I still remember them now. I liked Healy not because the school was good, but because I wanted to see my friends. For vacation, I usually just stayed home the entire summer. Sometimes I would go to a different state and stay there for about a week then come back. There wasn't anything very interesting. That's where Whitney Young came in. I found out that I passed the test and I got into Whitney. I wasn't that happy about saying goodbye to my friends but I didn't want to lose the chance.
